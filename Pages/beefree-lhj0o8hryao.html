WiseTech and Xero were the big players in a tech-led market slump on Monday after a Wall St sell-off raised growth concerns. The ASX 200 dropped 1.2 per cent to close 88 points lower at 7347 with all sectors down. The tech sector was down 4 per cent with WiseTech closing 7.3 per cent lower and Xero down more than 6 per cent. Good news from Qantas and Helloworld helped lift travel shares. Helloworld also provided a boost with an update signalling that the travel market continues to recover from COVID, which helped Webjet and FlightCentre close higher.
